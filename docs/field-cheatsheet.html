🛫 Pre-flight

1
Clean start. SA↑ SB↑ SC↑ SD↑, shoulders released. No switch warnings (SE/SF exempt).
2
GPS fix — OSD sats ≥ 8.
3
OSD check — battery V, video link, home-arrow present.
4
Launch assist? SD↓ before arming.
5
Arm — SA↓. Won't arm? Read the OSD "arming disabled" reason and fix it — don't force it.
6
Throttle up, throw.
7
Airborne — SD↑ so you don't re-enter launch.

🟢 Routine flight

  • Cruise → SB↑ ANGLE
  • Sportier → SB· ACRO
  • Expert → SB↓ MANUAL
  • Orbit / goggles → SC· LOITER
  • Find the plane → hold SE

🔴 Emergency

Lost orientation? SC↓ = RTH. Climbs to 50 m, flies home, then loiters — no auto-land. Take control (SC↑) to land. Dolphin never auto-lands.
Exiting RTH: set SB to ↑ first, then SC↑ — you drop straight into whatever SB holds.

⚖️ Who wins

  • RTH beats everything except ARM.
  • LOITER holds position; ANGLE/ACRO/MANUAL handle attitude underneath.
  • NAV LAUNCH lives only from arm → first climb-out, then self-exits.
  • No flight-mode aux = ACRO (implicit, SB·).

🛠️ Auto tune — do on Baloo / Dolphin, once

SF is live on all three planes, Swordfish included. Don't tap it airborne on Swordfish — it overwrites the known-good reference tune.
  1. Climb ≥ 80 m in ANGLE, calm air, open space.
  2. Tap SF → latch on (voice callout if configured). Plane induces pitch + roll oscillations.
  3. Fly figure-8s, let it sweep both axes 30–60 s.
  4. Tap SF → latch off. Saved to the active PID profile.
  5. Land. At the bench, disable AUTOTUNE in the aux block so mis-taps do nothing.
